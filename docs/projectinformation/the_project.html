The project has been initiated as a dissertational project with the title 'Prosopographia Memphitica. Individuelle Identitäten und Kollektive Biographien einer Residenzstadt des Neuen Reiches' in October 2015 and is supervised by Univ.-Prof. Dr. Jochem Kahl (Freie Universität Berlin) and PD Dr. Dietrich Raue (Universität Leipzig). From October 2015 to October 2018, the project was part of the TOPOI Research Group C1 - Deixis and Frames of Reference: Strategies of Perspectivation in Language, Text, and Image (see: https://www.topoi.org/project/c-1-x-10/). Persons: Anne Herzberg-Beiersdorf, M.A. – principal investigator Dr. Gordon Fischer & André Renis – IT und web support Institutions: Freie Universität Berlin Universität Leipzig Excellencecluster 264 TOPOI Berlin Graduate School of Ancient Studies
